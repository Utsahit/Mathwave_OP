# Elixir & Oak — Customer Guide

**Website:** `https://app.elixirandoak.com`

---

## 1. Browsing the Menu

- Visit the website and browse our full menu
- Items are organized by category (Starters, Mains, Desserts, Drinks)
- Each item shows a photo, description, price, and dietary tags
- Use the search bar to find specific items

---

## 2. Placing an Order

1. Browse menu items
2. Click **Add to Cart** on items you want
3. Review your cart (tap the cart icon)
4. Adjust quantities as needed
5. Click **Proceed to Checkout**
6. Enter delivery or pickup details
7. Choose payment method
8. Click **Place Order**

You'll receive a confirmation with your order number.

**Tip:** Create an account to save your favorites and addresses for faster checkout!

---

## 3. Tracking Your Order

After placing an order, you can track its status in real-time:

```
Order Placed → Confirmed → Being Prepared → Ready → On its Way → Delivered
```

- Status updates appear on the order page
- You'll receive notifications at each step
- Estimated delivery time is shown

---

## 4. Making a Reservation

1. Click **Reservations**
2. Select date, time, and party size
3. Add any special requests (birthday, allergies, etc.)
4. Confirm your reservation

You'll receive a confirmation. To modify or cancel, visit the Reservations page.

---

## 5. Payments

We accept:
- **Credit/Debit Cards** (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- **UPI** (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- **Net Banking**
- **Wallets** (Paytm Wallet, Mobikwik)
- **Cash** (at the restaurant)

All payments are processed securely through Razorpay. We never store your card details.

---

## 6. Loyalty Program

Join our loyalty program to earn points with every order!

### How it works:
- Earn 1 point for every ₹100 spent
- 100 points = ₹50 discount on your next order
- Bonus points on your birthday
- Exclusive offers for loyalty members

### How to join:
1. Create an account
2. Start ordering — points are automatically tracked
3. Check your points balance on your profile

---

## 7. Coupons & Gift Cards

### Using a Coupon:
1. At checkout, enter the coupon code
2. Discount is applied automatically
3. Only one coupon per order

### Using a Gift Card:
1. At checkout, select "Gift Card" as payment
2. Enter the gift card code
3. Amount is deducted from your balance
