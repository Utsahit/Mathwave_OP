Everything you need to know before you commit. Still not sure? Message us on WhatsApp — we respond fast.

Can I freeze my membership?
Yes. Monthly members can freeze for up to 2 months per year. Annual members can freeze for up to 3 months. A small admin fee of ₹299 applies.
Is there a joining fee?
There is a one-time registration fee of ₹999 for all memberships. This covers your biometric setup, welcome kit, and initial fitness assessment.
Can I bring a guest?
Annual members receive 2 guest passes per month. Monthly members can purchase guest day passes at ₹599 each. Day pass holders cannot bring guests.
How do I book personal training?
Browse our trainers online and book directly via WhatsApp. Our front desk team can also help you match with the right trainer for your goals.
