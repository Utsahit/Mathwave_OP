Our Story

Where the City
Comes to Breathe

Born in the monsoon of 2019, The Bombay Brew House is a love letter to Mumbai — specifically to the Bandra that smells of street food and sea breeze, where Art Deco arches frame chaotic, beautiful streets.

We drew our soul from the grand Irani cafés of Colaba and Mohammed Ali Road — high ceilings, marble counters, ceiling fans that move like slow sighs — and stirred in the restless energy of a city that never truly sleeps.

When the monsoon arrives, our windows fog up and the scent of chai, wet earth, and baking bread mingles into something ineffable. That moment — that is why we exist.
