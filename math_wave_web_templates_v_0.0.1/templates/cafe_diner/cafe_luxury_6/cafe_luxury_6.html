Our Story

01

Behind the Brass Door

"The best conversations happen in rooms where the world outside is briefly forgotten."

Tucked behind a nondescript brass door on Residency Road, The Vault was conceived as an antidote to the loud and the obvious. We believe a great evening is earned slowly — through a perfectly balanced Negroni, a dish that surprises you, and a playlist that understands the room.

Our kitchen is led by Chef Arjun Menon, whose background spans Bombay Canteen, Noma Copenhagen, and the backyards of his grandmother in Kozhikode. The bar programme is crafted by mixologist Zara Khan — previously of Sidecar, Delhi.
