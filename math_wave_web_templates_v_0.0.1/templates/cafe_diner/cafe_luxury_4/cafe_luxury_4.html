Filter Coffee Culture, Reimagined

Kaapi didn't die. It evolved. We pour single-origin beans through traditional stainless steel filters, then cold-brew them overnight and serve them over river-stone ice. The same ritual Mysore grandmothers swear by — but with beans sourced from Chikmagalur's finest micro-lots. Because Bangalore never discards its roots. It just upgrades them.

After the Cycling Loop

Sunday mornings on Indiranagar's streets belong to the cyclists who wind through the leafy avenues before the tech crowd wakes. We open at 7 AM with you in mind. Park your bike, claim a table under the ficus canopy, and let a jackfruit slider and a cold brew restore what Cubbon Park took from your legs. Cleats and jerseys are always welcome here.

The Perfect Laptop-Friendly Canopy

Bangalore runs on sprints and standups. Between deployments and design reviews, you need a place where the WiFi is faster than your tech debt and the ambient sound is birdsong, not a barista calling a name. Our canopy tables come with USB-C outlets, zero time-limits, and a no-judgment policy for that third cold brew. Deep work deserves deep green.
